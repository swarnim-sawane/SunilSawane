About Me

I am Sunil Sawane, an artist whose work is deeply rooted in the harmony of nature, human emotions, and the beauty of life. My journey in art has spanned over two decades, and during this time, I have been particularly drawn to the intricate and expressive style of pen and ink. My compositions, often inspired by the natural world, are crafted with precision using pen on paper, featuring prominent figures of leaves, birds, animals, and flowers.

From the soothing grace of "Fantasy" to the evocative expressions of maternal love in nature, my paintings reflect the world as I experience it. I strive to blend cultural heritage with innovative techniques, setting new trends in contemporary art. Each piece is a milestone in my artistic journey, inviting you to explore the depth and beauty that I see in life.
